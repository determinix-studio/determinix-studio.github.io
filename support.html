Common Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the game and navigate to Settings, then tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same App Store or Google Play account used for the original purchase.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed through Apple or Google. For iOS, visit reportaproblem.apple.com. For Android, open the Google Play Store and go to your purchase history.

How do I delete my data?

Email us at support@determinix.com with your request. Please include the device type and any relevant purchase information so we can locate your data.
